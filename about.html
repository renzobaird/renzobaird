Obtaining a college degree was incredibly important to me. Despite the time that passed, I never gave up.

The Story, the journey, the purpose.

I am fascinated by human's way of thinking and their relationship with technology. I look for ways to use technology to enhance the lives of my users that feel memorable.

With every design I make, I always ask myself- what emotion do I want my user to feel? Does the current version of the design meet that goal? What can I bring into the design that builds a stronger relationship between the user and the intended experience?

Every design choice of mine is intentional - stemming from my design principles: Provoke thought and provide meaning, encourage engagement, use feedback to iterate and improve, make design decisions backed by data, and look for ways to enhance the intended experience.

I care about authenticity and creativity. A user should experience a product that feels new, captivating, and emergent. My focus is always on creating work that is immersive, innovative, intentional, and inclusive.

ABOUT ME

INSPIRATIONS

I am fascinated by human’s way of thinking and their relationship with technology. I look for ways to use technology to enhance the lives of my users that feel memorable.

With every design I make, I always ask myself- what emotion do I want my user to feel? Does the current version of the design meet that goal? What can I introduce into the design that builds a stronger relationship between the user and the intended experience?

Every design choice of mine is intentional, stemming from my core design principles: Provoke thought and provide meaning, encourage engagement, use feedback to iterate and improve, make design decisions backed by data (or let data drive design decisions), and look for ways to solidify the intended experience and emotions of the user.

I care about authenticity, creativity, immersive narratives, and world building. Emergent gameplay that feels new and captivating.

It is with everything above that I set out my intentions to make a difference in the world through innovative, memorable, and inclusive designs.
