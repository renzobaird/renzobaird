Game Design

Obtaining a college degree was incredibly important to me. Despite the time that passed, I never gave up on my dreams of a college education.

In previous years I explored educational paths towards psychology and computer science. While gaining life experience over the years, I decided to change course one final time. By pursuing my interest in design, I smashed the two previous paths with design to land where I am today.

I am a student in the Arts and Entertainment Technologies (AET) program at The University of Texas at Austin. With a concentration of Game Design, my Bachelor of Science in AET will be earned in May 2026.

The Story, the journey, the purpose.

I am fascinated by human's way of thinking and their relationship with technology. I look for ways to use technology to enhance the lives of my users that feel memorable.

With every design I make, I always ask myself- what emotion do I want my user to feel? Does the current version of the design meet that goal? What can I introduce into the design that builds a stronger relationship between the user and the intended experience?

Every design choice of mine is intentional, stemming from my core design principles: Provoke thought and provide meaning, encourage engagement, use feedback to iterate and improve, make design decisions backed by data, and look for ways to solidify the intended experience and emotions of the user.

I care about authenticity, creativity, immersive narratives, and world building. Emergent gameplay that feels new and captivating. It is with everything above that I set out my intentions to make a difference in the world through innovative, memorable, and inclusive designs.

ABOUT ME

INSPIRATIONS

I am fascinated by human’s way of thinking and their relationship with technology. I look for ways to use technology to enhance the lives of my users that feel memorable.

With every design I make, I always ask myself- what emotion do I want my user to feel? Does the current version of the design meet that goal? What can I introduce into the design that builds a stronger relationship between the user and the intended experience?

Every design choice of mine is intentional, stemming from my core design principles: Provoke thought and provide meaning, encourage engagement, use feedback to iterate and improve, make design decisions backed by data (or let data drive design decisions), and look for ways to solidify the intended experience and emotions of the user.

I care about authenticity, creativity, immersive narratives, and world building. Emergent gameplay that feels new and captivating.

It is with everything above that I set out my intentions to make a difference in the world through innovative, memorable, and inclusive designs.

SPECIALIZATIONS
